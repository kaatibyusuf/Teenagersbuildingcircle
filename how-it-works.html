From registration to your child's first live class.

Nothing here depends on a fixed appointment or waiting on us, and every step exists for a specific reason.

1

Register

A short form with the parent's details and the child's, including preferred intake and anything facilitators should know in advance. Takes a few minutes, online.

A Few Minutes
2

Sign the Code of Conduct

Both the student and the parent read and agree to a short, specific conduct agreement, attendance, respect, and online etiquette, before the first live session. Two signatures, not one.

Student & Parent
3

Pay the First Month

₦30,000 per child, paid to the account details sent after registration. This single payment covers the diagnostic assessment and everything that follows for that month.

₦30,000
4

Complete the Diagnostic Assessment

Sent the moment payment is confirmed. An honest, untimed assessment covering academics, deen, hifdh, practical skills, and daily habits, reading, screen time, time management. Roughly forty five minutes to an hour, completed whenever suits your family over the following days.

~1 Hour, Self-Paced
5

Placement Review

Facilitators review the assessment and place your child individually in each pillar, Foundational, Intermediate, or Advanced, based on where they actually stand, not their age. A child can sit at different levels in different pillars at once.

Per-Pillar Placement
6

Slack Workspace Onboarding

Your child is added to their cohort's Slack workspace, with separate channels for each pillar, and separate cohorts by gender. You're added to a parent summary channel where attendance and progress are visible each week.

Cohort Assigned
7

The Weekly Rhythm Begins

Live sessions, reading, and reflection start on the recurring weekly schedule below, and continue through a twelve-week term.

Beyond the First Week

The weekly rhythm above repeats across a full term, building toward a review rather than a single exam.

Weeks 1–6
Foundation

Placement takes effect and each pillar begins building from the student's actual starting point.

Week 7
Mid-Term Check

Facilitators review progress across all four pillars and adjust pace where needed.

Weeks 8–11
Depth

Each pillar builds further, with a capstone task or project taking shape by pillar.

Week 12
Term Review

A certificate is issued, and each pillar's level is reviewed for the next term.

If something comes up mid-term, a missed session, a school exam clash, Ramadan, withdrawing early, these are all covered in the Parent FAQ, along with what happens to fees in each case. Read the Parent FAQ →
